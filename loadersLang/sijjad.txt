Here is a poem for Sijjad Khan:

Sijjad Khan
In circuits of code and dreams made real,
Sijjad Khan stands, with a steady zeal.
A software engineer, fingers that weave
Worlds from nothing, making ideas breathe.
Lines of logic dance at his command,
Bugs bow down, systems understand.
From dawn till dusk, through complex streams,
He crafts solutions like woven dreams.
A heart of gold in a silicon age,
Kindness flows through every page.
A very good soul, gentle and true,
Helping others as he pushes through.
God-gifted skills, a rare, bright flame,
He can do everything, yet stays the same—
Humble in triumph, wise in his way,
Turning challenges into light of day.
Sijjad Khan, with vision so clear,
Engineer of wonders, far and near.
In every line of code, in every deed,
A quiet hero planting hopeful seed.
May your keys keep clicking, your heart stay light,
May success and joy walk by your side.
For the world is brighter with talents like yours—
Sijjad Khan, the one who opens doors.
